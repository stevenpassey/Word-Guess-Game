Hangman Mode

      Hangman mode is the default mode. It is the mode that follows the homework grading rubric. In Hangman mode, the player chooses letters for the word in any order. The round is over when the player has made 9 wrong guesses, or the player has correctly guessed all the letters. Typist Mode

      Typist mode is a faster mode than hangman mode. In Typist mode, the player chooses letters for the word in order from left to right. The round is over when the player presses enter. The correct word is displayed in green if the player is correct, and red if the player incorrect. 24 hangman  | 48 typist   
